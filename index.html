What's going on here?

You find yourself faced with the insufferable Mrs. Bingham. She is known for cutting remarks and thinks herself more important than others. It is up to you to take her down. Use insults and biting remarks to take her down, utilize clever comebacks to heal your hurt pride and reputation. If Mrs. Bingham gets you down too much, you will automatically commit a charitable act which will grant you a boost in reputation, allowing you another turn (an extra life so to speak. Beware, this is your last chance!)
